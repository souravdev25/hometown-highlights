Welcome to Barishal - The Venice of Bengal

Barishal, also known as Barisal, is a picturesque city located in southern Bangladesh. It is famous for its rivers, canals, and lush green landscapes. Known as the "Venice of Bengal," Barishal is a hub of culture, history, and natural beauty. The city is also renowned for its delicious cuisine and warm-hearted people.

Historical Places in Barishal

  1. Guthia Mosque
  2. Durga Sagar
  3. Oxford Mission Church
  4. Bell's Park

Popular Foods of Barishal

  • Kachki Fish Curry
  • Chui Jhal Morich (Spicy Chili Dish)
  • Panta Bhat (Fermented Rice)
  • Barishal's Special Pitha (Rice Cakes)
